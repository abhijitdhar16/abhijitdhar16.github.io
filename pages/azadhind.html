Azad Hind

The name Azad Hind means independent India. INA (Indian Nation Army) was an armed force formed by Indian nationalists in 1942 in Southeast Asia during World War II with the help of Japan and Germany. Their aim was to azad (independent or free) India from British rule. These stamps were printed in the Nazi Germany in the name of new country to be born. INA was defeated and these stamps were never used.
